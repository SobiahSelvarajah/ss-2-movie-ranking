The Best Movies According to Sobiah

My top 3 movies of all-time.


Pearl Harbor

War based rom-com starring Ben Affleck, Josh Hartnett and Kate Beckinsale.


A Walk To Remember

Definitely one of Mandy Moore's best movies, absolute tear jerker and worth the watch!


Sanam Teri Kasam

A modern day Bollywood version of the book Love Story by Eric Segal.
